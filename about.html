About Dan's Bakery

Baking happiness since 2010

Our Story

Dan’s Bakery was founded with one goal in mind: to bring freshly baked goods to the heart of the community. From humble beginnings in a small kitchen, Dan’s passion for baking quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood bakery.

Our Mission

To serve high-quality, delicious baked treats made with love and locally sourced ingredients. We aim to create memorable moments with every bite.
